What You Can Do
Full breakdown of licensee permissions — what's yours to control, what CrewBox manages.
✅ What You Can Do — Full Control
🏢
Add, manage, and remove clients
Onboard new contractors in 3 minutes. Pause or deactivate accounts instantly. View every client's full dashboard and activity.
FULL ACCESS
💵
Set your own prices
Charge your clients whatever price the market will bear. $300, $500, $800/month — your call. The spread between what you charge and what you pay CrewBox is your profit.
FULL CONTROL
🎨
Brand everything as your own
Your logo, your colors, your domain, your email sender name. Every touchpoint your clients see carries your brand. CrewBox is completely invisible to them.
FULL WHITE-LABEL
🤖
Configure every AI agent per client
Customize voice, hours, transfer rules, reminder timing, social platforms, and special instructions for each contractor individually.
FULL CONFIG
📊
See all client data and activity
Every call transcript, every invoice, every payment, every review — for all your clients. Full visibility across your entire book of business.
FULL VISIBILITY
📜
Access all sales materials
Cold outreach scripts, objection handling, demo guides, pricing calculators — everything you need to close new clients is built into the platform.
INCLUDED
📱
Pause or activate any agent
Toggle any of the 5 AI agents on or off for any client at any time. Useful during transitions, holidays, or when a client wants to handle something manually.
INSTANT CONTROL
💳
Manage your own billing
Update your payment method, view invoices, upgrade your plan, and download receipts anytime from the billing section.
SELF-SERVICE
🔒 What You Cannot Do — Platform Level
🏗️
Modify the core AI models
The underlying AI (Claude, Vapi) is managed by CrewBox. You configure behavior and personality — you don't change the model itself.
PLATFORM ONLY
💸
Access contractor payment accounts
Your clients' Stripe Connected Accounts and bank details are private to them. You see aggregated revenue data, not individual financial accounts.
PRIVACY PROTECTED
🔁
Sub-license to other operators
Building your own reseller network requires the Enterprise tier. On Growth, you sell directly to contractors — not to other licensees.
ENTERPRISE ONLY
📞
Listen to live calls in progress
You can read call transcripts and summaries after calls end, but you cannot monitor calls in real time. Contractor privacy is protected.
PRIVACY PROTECTED
📧
Access contractor customer contact lists
Customer data belongs to the contractor. You see aggregate stats (total customers, call count) but not individual customer phone numbers or emails.
DATA OWNERSHIP
🗄️
Export raw database records
Raw bulk data export is not available on any tier. Reports and analytics are available in-platform. Individual contractors can export their own data.
NOT AVAILABLE
